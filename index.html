Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Powerful capture modes, a full annotation suite, and smart workflows — all wrapped in a Liquid Glass interface.

Smart Capture

Region select, fullscreen, active window, screen recording, and GIF capture — all with customizable global hotkeys.

Annotation Editor

Pen, rectangles, ellipses, arrows, text overlays, and blur tool. Draw on any screenshot with full precision before sharing.

Video Annotator

Scrub through recordings, annotate individual frames, extract frame PNGs, or export full annotated videos with drawings baked in.

Capture History

Every capture is logged with thumbnails, timestamps, and upload status. Search, filter, and reopen any past capture instantly.

Multi-Destination Upload

Upload to Imgur, custom HTTP endpoints, or save to disk — all in parallel. One failure never blocks the others.

Cross-Platform

Native experience on both Windows and macOS. Custom title bar, system tray, dark/light theme with OS-level integration.
